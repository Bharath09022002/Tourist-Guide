Tamil Nadu is India's well-known tourist destination loved for its splendour, beauty, rich culture as well as religious significance. Tamil Nadu tourism boasts loads of pilgrimage sites that attract tourists from all over the globe.

In addition to the long history of the Tamil people, Tamil Nadu is famous for its temples, festivals, and celebration of the arts. The Hindu temples and monuments at Mamallapuram have become famous tourist attractions.

It is managed by the Tourism,Culture and Religious Endowments Department. The tag line "Enchanting Tamil Nadu" was adopted in the tourism promotions.
